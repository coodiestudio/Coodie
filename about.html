Coodie Studio was born from a simple frustration: most websites are bad. Slow, bloated, templated, and forgettable. We decided to do something about it.

We work with brands who understand that their website is an asset — not an afterthought. Every pixel, every interaction, every line of code is intentional.

Our approach is brutally direct: clear brief → clean architecture → ruthless execution. No endless revision cycles. No creative by committee. Just results.

We write vanilla code because we believe performance is a design decision. A fast website isn't a bonus — it's the baseline. Everything else is architecture on top of that.

THE
PROCESS_

01_

Discovery

We start by understanding your brand, your audience, and what success looks like for you. A focused brief that eliminates ambiguity and sets the foundation for everything that follows.

02_

Architecture

Sitemap, wireframes, content hierarchy. We map the entire experience before any visual design begins, ensuring every decision serves the user and the goal.

03_

High-fidelity mockups that define the complete visual language: typography, color, spacing, motion. One round of revision. No creative dilution through endless feedback loops.

Custom-coded from scratch. Pixel-perfect, performant, and fully responsive. Every interaction is engineered, not just placed. We test across browsers and devices before anything goes live.
