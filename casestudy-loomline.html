// 01 THE_BRIEF

From zero presence
to market-ready.

LOOMLINE arrived with a clear product and a clear ambition: break into the European luxury home market with a digital presence that could stand alongside names with decades of heritage. What they didn't have was time — or a template that could do it justice.

The brief was precise: a single-page experience that tells the brand story, establishes premium positioning, and converts visitors into leads — all in one seamless scroll. No fluff. No padding. Just a site that earns trust the moment it loads.

// The Challenge

Breaking in with nothing but quality

Zero brand recognition in a saturated luxury segment. Competitors with 20-year histories and established visual identities. A launch deadline that couldn't move. European buyers who evaluate credibility in seconds and don't give second chances.

// Our Approach

Lead with story. Close with architecture.

We designed a one-page journey that made the product the hero: an editorial-grade layout that communicates craftsmanship before the user reads a word. Every section was engineered to carry the visitor forward — until they had no reason not to get in touch.

// 02 THE_PROCESS

How we
built it.

Every Coodie project follows the same discipline: brief first, architecture second, design third, code last. No visual decisions are made before the strategy is locked. No code is written before the design is approved. Every week has a deliverable. Every deliverable has a purpose.

STEP 01

Brand Immersion

An extended brief session to extract brand DNA: who they're for, what they stand for, how they want to be perceived, and what "success" looks like in 12 months. No design starts without this clarity.

STEP 02

UX Architecture

Before a pixel was placed, we mapped the conversion journey. Which sections build trust? Where does the visitor's intent peak? Where does the CTA land for maximum conversion? Only then did we define the page structure.

STEP 03

Editorial Visual Design

A full desktop + mobile design system: custom typography hierarchy, luxury whitespace proportions, colour palette derived from the product, and micro-interaction specs. Every choice justified against the brief.

STEP 04

Custom Development

Hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No WordPress, no page builders, no third-party bloat. The result is a site that loads fast, renders perfectly on every device, and is entirely owned by the client.

STEP 05

SEO & Launch Optimisation

Schema markup, meta structure, Open Graph tags, performance audit, and cross-browser QA — all before the domain went live. The site was indexable and conversion-ready from day one.
