// 07 — RULES

POST
GUIDELINES_

01
Background

Use pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff) backgrounds only. No gradients, no textures, no photos behind text.

Dark posts → white body text
Light posts → black body text
Handles always in Blue (#5BC8F5)

02
Typography

Headlines: Archivo Black, uppercase, tight letter-spacing (−0.03em). Body/captions: Inter, sentence case.

H1 size: 13–18vw
H2 size: 8–12vw
Caption: 0.8–1rem Inter

03
Highlights

Max 1–3 words highlighted per slide. Use rectangular background blocks (no border-radius). Alternate Yellow/Blue across the carousel.

Slide 1 → Yellow
Slide 2 → Blue
Slide 3 → no highlight or Yellow
Slide 4 → both (numbered list)

04
Anatomy

Every slide has 4 fixed elements: slide number (top left), asterisk ✳ (top right), @coodie.studio handle (bottom left), arrow → (bottom right).

Slide num: 0.75rem Inter grey
Asterisk: 1.5rem bold
Handle: Archivo Black blue
Arrow: subtle grey

05
Spacing

Generous white space. Headline content is vertically centred. Footer elements always at the bottom. Padding: ~2rem on all sides.

Post ratio: 4:5 (portrait)
Content area: centre-aligned vertically
Never crowd the frame

06
Carousel Structure

Typical carousel: Hook (dark) → Truth (light) → Problem expand (dark) → Solution list (light) → Proof/CTA (dark). Max 7 slides.

Alternate dark / light backgrounds
End on a strong CTA slide
Last slide = contact / @handle
