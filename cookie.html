1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and improve user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for basic functionality, such as saving your language preferences or enabling contact forms.We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party cookies (e.g., Meta, Google)

3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block or delete cookies
  • Receive alerts before a cookie is stored
Disabling cookies may affect some parts of the website’s functionality.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as necessary. Any updates will be posted on this page.
